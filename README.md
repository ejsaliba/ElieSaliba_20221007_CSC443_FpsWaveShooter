FpsWaveShooter

Controls:
WASD for movement
Left click to shoot
Spacebar to jump
T to change weapons
Q to use healing ability

Menus
W/S to cycle selected
Enter to confirm selected

Enemy Types:
Melee enemy-Basic close range/high health enemy   20 POINTS
Ranged enemy-Shoots a projectile/low health   30 POINTS
Explosive enemy-Fast enemy that self destructs damaging everything in its radius/low health-high damage   50 POINTS

Upgrades:
Double Jump Upgrade- allows the user to jump again mid air for mobility
Machine Gun- unlocks the machine gun weapon- fast low damage automatic weapon
Sniper Rifle- unlocks the sniper weapon- slow high damage weapon
Rocket Launcher- unlocks the rocket launcher weapon- slow projectile weapon that deals huge area damage with low ammo
Ammo refill- refills all unlocked weapons' ammo

I am proud of the way i set up my waves and shop, the rotating maps give the player some variety, and the scaling of the waves and shop system was fun to work on, although i do wish i adjusted the price of the upgrades.
